About

If you had told me ten years ago I'd end up getting into coding, I would've been surprised. I studied biology in school, and spent most of post-college years doing on-again, off-again fieldwork for different biological studies. By some kind of luck, I ended up working at a startup in San Francisco, and I discovered there that I really enjoyed being in the tech sphere. Only after I'd left San Francisco did it occur to me to try my hand at coding.

I'm still a total neophyte, and I'm humbled every day - but my goal right now is to become an honest-to-goodness full-stack generalist. I get a real kick out of seeing features through from one end to the other, and I like being able to use what I've made. I'm looking for opportunities anywhere where people are willing to take a shot on someone who doesn't know much, but learns fast.

Herc Grades

Herc Grades is an app that makes it easy for teachers to keep grades on daily assignments.

  • Teachers can log assignments and assign grades alphabetically or by custom seating chart.
  • Students or parents can log in to check their grades as soon as they've been logged.
  • Grades will sync up automatically with Canvas - a popular learning management system.
